MESSAGE FROM PRESIDENT GMS

My Dear Brothers and Sisters,
Jai Mohyal

I hope all my Mohyal brothers and sisters are in good health and doing great in this New Year. As we welcome the vibrant festival of Basant Panchami, symbolizing knowledge, prosperity, and the arrival of spring, I extend my heartfelt greetings to all Mohyal brothers and sisters. May Maa Saraswati bless our Community with wisdom and enlightenment.
January has been a cold month and to ensure the continuity of our efforts and discussions despite the weather, we conducted an online meeting to address ongoing matters of importance.
Looking ahead, I am pleased to announce an upcoming meeting at the GMS office in February. I earnestly request all Presidents and Secretaries of all the affiliated Mohyal Sabhas to attend this meeting. Together, we will address significant issues concerning widow empowerment and education for Mohyal children. Not even a single Mohyal child should be deprived of education, because of insufficient funds. Also we will discuss various ways and methods to empower the widows of our Community, so that they can have a stable and dignified life. These are critical areas where we must channel our collective efforts to uplift and support underprivileged members of our Community.
Let us continue to work with dedication and try to live up to our shared vision of ensuring no member of our Mohyal family is left behind. I look forward to your valuable inputs and participation. Wishing good health and happiness to each one of you.

Yours affectionately,

Vinod Kumar Dutt
President GMS
